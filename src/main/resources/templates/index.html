Description

A little gist of the what and the why

What exactly is a hidden message wordsearch?

It is exactly the same as a regular wordsearch except for one final gift: when all the words have been found, the untouched letters, when read up to down, right to left, spell a hidden message. These wordsearches are perfect for gifts, scavenger hunts, or even just for fun!

Why is this hidden message algorithm cool?

To understand why this seemingly simple wordsearch algorithm is cool, I urge you to try and create a randomized algorithm that takes in words and a hidden message and creates a wordsearch such that the remaining letters spell exactly the hidden message. It's tougher than it seems, and the internet would agree. Currently, no existing algorithm really works! Most leave leftover random letters after the hidden message, and some error more than half the time.
